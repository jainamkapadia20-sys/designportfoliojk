ABOUT ME

Designing the interface between people and machines.

I’m an Experience Design student at Flame University currently in my 2nd Year, I have a keen interest in learning about Interactive Systems Design & Mobility Systems. While most see cars as a way to get from A to B, I see it as a massive design challenge: how do we keep the "soul" of driving alive in an increasingly automated world?

I feel that cars and their systems fascinate me a lot, especially since I own a tuned car, I know the feeling of the power it has. It somehow motivates me to keep learning about them.
